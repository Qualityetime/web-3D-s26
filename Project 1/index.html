Omniflicks is a wrist gauntlet that works like a photo scanner. You point at an object, tap the screen, and it captures a photo, analyzes what you captured, and tells you what it is. I wanted it to feel sleek, minimalistic, and calm, like a tool that can replace constantly pulling out your phone. The 3D model focuses on materials and lighting to make it feel real, and the interface allows you to customize the device as if you are purchasing it.
